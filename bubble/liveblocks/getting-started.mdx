This guide will help you get started with installation, setup, and building real-time collaborative features like presence and live cursors in your Bubble app.

## What is Liveblocks?

[Liveblocks](https://liveblocks.io/) is a platform that allows users to add collaborative features to their products, such as comments, notifications, and real-time APIs.

## What's in the plugin?

The free Liveblocks plugin implements a small portion of what is possible with Liveblocks and is maintained by [Lunch Pail Labs](https://www.lunchpaillabs.com). With [Liveblocks](https://liveblocks.io), users can:

- See who is online and track their activity with **presence**.
- Watch each other’s **live cursor** movements across the page in real-time.
- Create and update **Liveblocks rooms** directly from within Bubble, enabling dynamic, shared experiences for your users.

## Installation and Setup

Follow these steps to install and configure the Liveblocks plugin:

1. **Prerequisites**:  
   Ensure you have a [Liveblocks account](https://liveblocks.io/signup) and a [Bubble account](https://bubble.io/login?mode=signup).

2. **Install the Plugin**:  
   Navigate to the [Bubble Plugin Marketplace](https://bubble.io/plugin/liveblocks-by-lunch-pail-labs-1717457909317x496158240134922200) and install the Liveblocks plugin into your Bubble app.

3. **Verify Installation**:  
   Confirm that the Liveblocks plugin appears in your list of installed plugins in Bubble.

4. **Input Plugin Keys**:  
   In the Liveblocks plugin settings on your plugins tab, add your **Liveblocks Public API Key** from the [Liveblocks dashboard](https://liveblocks.io/dashboard/).
